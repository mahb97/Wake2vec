### The bet

the wager is that 8B P2 walls fast, like 3B did. the reasoning, from the 8B P1 analysis:

1. **8B P1 plateaued** at val 11.48 and showed the most extreme train-val divergence in the lineup. limited capacity to descend was already its signature.
2. **The compositional-init geometry.** the 8B's Wake embeddings integrated *into* the base manifold (Cohen's d -1.25), where 3B's spherical embeddings sat on a distinct shell. P2 freezes those embeddings and asks LoRA to route them. if the wall is about how routable the embedding configuration is, the integrated embeddings may wall as fast as or faster than 3B's, because the Wake region is not geometrically distinct from base for the attention routing to latch onto.

if the bet holds, the run dies early (like 3B's 600) and the slow 240s/step never compounds into the 200-hour full-run projection. if it surprises and descends past step 150 (like Qwen), the bet was wrong, the LoRA ceiling is scale-gated beyond Qwen alone, and the run drops to SEQ_LEN 256 for the long haul.

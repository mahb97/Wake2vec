### Three findings in one eval

1. **Geometric null, confirmed at 10%.** L_morph reads 0.0006 across all four batches at step 100, identical to step 0 and step 50. λ=50 produces zero observable movement in 100 steps. TinyLlama P3 took 1000 steps to establish this. Llama 1B P3 took 1000. 3B P3 strong settled it in 100. The fastest null in the lineup.

2. **L_device is noise.** Range 0.19 to 0.21 across batches, no monotonic trend, no triplet convergence forming. Identical behaviour to Llama 1B P3's random walk (0.185 to 0.210). The device-clustering objective is structurally unlearnable, not just on TinyLlama (32K vocab) and not just on Llama 1B (128K), but now also on Llama 3B (128K). Three architectures, same null.

3. **NEW: LM degradation under strong lambdas.** Val 5.61 vs P2's wall at 5.33 is a +0.28 increase. P3 strong is not just failing to move the auxiliary metrics, but is *actively pulling LoRA weights away from the LM-optimal P2 configuration*. The auxiliary objectives drag the model off its P2 minimum without producing the geometric structure they're asking for and the cost shows up as LM regression.

### current arg: 

*strong auxiliary geometric pressure (λ_morph=50, λ_device=2) on 128K-vocab Llama at 3B parameters fails to move targeted geometric metrics AND degrades language modelling as a cost*. the loss landscape actively penalises the wrong objective rather than ignoring it.

1B P3 showed indifference while the 3B P3 shows the landscape pushing back.

### Timing and early-stop projection

EARLY_STOP_PATIENCE=5 at 100-step eval cadence means termination after 5 evals without val improvement. realistic end point is step 500 to 700.

## Notes

Phi-3 Mini launches as soon as 3B P3 terminates.

1. **Phi extends the 58% Wake-vocab-share cluster.** currently TinyLlama (58%) and Mistral (58%) sit there alone but Phi's 32K vocab puts it in the same cluster. third datapoint makes the smaller-model paradox a pattern across three architectures (TinyLlama base, Mistral SWA, Phi dense), not a TinyLlama/Mistral coincidence.

2. **"Textbook quality training data" is a new hypothesis.** Phi-3 was trained on filtered high-signal data designed for clean reasoning but the Wake is the corpus at maximum distance from that filter. does a model trained for cleanliness resist embedding injection that asks it to internalise chaos? if Phi generates Wake-style prose as densely as TinyLlama did, evidence the embedding injection overrides pretraining bias. if it doesn't, evidence pretraining priors gate what injection can do. 

Gemma 2 9B launches second. it extends architectural coverage (Google RMSNorm placement, gated activations) and confirms the high-vocab-low-injection pattern Qwen already shows.

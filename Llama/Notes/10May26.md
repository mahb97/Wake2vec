## DGX Spark detour

acknowledging the compute fantasy that's been playing in my mind, the relevant specs:

| Constraint | T4 (current) | DGX Spark |
|------------|--------------|-----------|
| VRAM | 15 GB | 128 GB unified |
| Disconnect risk | Every ~80 steps | Zero |
| Session count for Qwen P1 | 30+ | One continuous run |
| Largest model that fits at 4-bit | ~14B | ~70B comfortably |
| SEQ_LEN ceiling | 128 (Qwen), 256 (Mistral) | 2048+ |

what that actually means for the project:
- **Llama 3.1-70B** becomes possible and is 17x bigger than my current largest Llama.
- **Qwen 2.5-72B** becomes possible and could then test whether the smaller model paradox holds at extreme scale.
- **Continuous P1 to P2 to P3** without resume infrastructure, no STEP_OFFSET, no SentryMirror, bye bye checkpoints.
- **Full attention windows** at SEQ_LEN 2048+, could capture multi-paragraph Wake patterns (parenthetical nesting, structural mimicry across pages).
- **Multiple models simultaneously.** no more "Tuesday is Qwen day, Wednesday is Mistral day," and how many google accounts can i create to acceess the T4s on colab.

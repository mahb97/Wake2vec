### recapping the 8B P1 findings

The 8B has no floor. It's spike wall-to-wall, and it breaks words in BPE stumps: "himsel," "befor," "firs," "prope," "throug," "wher," (words cut at token boundaries). The vocabulary floods in from the whole pretraining prior: PhpStorm, _SERVER, GetMethod, 
Benghazi, Milwaukee, four scripts at once. In the Wake, Joyce breaks words where meaning divides but the 8B breaks them where the tokenizer divides. The 8B has literalised the level-mismatch thesis and thus deforms at the level the model computes over instead 
of the level the Wake operates at.

TinyLlama at low temperature relaxes back toward coherent list-English, but the 8B stays fragmented. The damage is in the mode of the distribution. The 8B learned that "Wake" means broken, and internalised the statistics of damage rather than the 
grammar of deformation. the 8B's wider pretraining prior is exactly what it collapses into when finetuning destabilises it, where more capacity means more junk within reach. TinyLlama's narrow prior acts as a regulariser; it can't stray far from English, 
so deformation stays local and the voice stays above water.

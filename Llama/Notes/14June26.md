### The three P3-strong findings, finalised

1. **Geometric null, confirmed at the fastest pace in the lineup.** L_morph 0.0006 across every [LOSS] printout from step 0 to step 450. λ=50 moved nothing across 450 steps. TinyLlama and Llama 1B each took 1000 steps to establish this; 3B settled it by step 100 and held it for the rest.

2. **L_device structurally unlearnable, confirmed third time.** Random walk 0.18-0.21, no triplet convergence. The 0.998 isotropy of the Wake region (confirmed in Qwen P1 canonical too) explains why: triplets can't cluster on a near-uniform sphere.

3. **The lasting cost of strong λs is the generalisation gap, not LM fit.** Train-val gap widened from 0.09 (P2) to 0.74 (P3 step 200) to 0.85 (P3 step 400). Val recovers toward the wall; the embedding configuration under aux pressure overfits training blocks in a way the unconstrained P2 config doesn't, and the overfitting grows with training time. This is the nuanced third finding that replaced both the original "active degradation" reading (wrong) and the "no lasting effect" reading (incomplete).

## Notes

850 to P1 end. Mistral is the project's critical test of the refined smaller-model paradox:

- **58% Wake vocab share** (matches TinyLlama, both at 32K base + ~44K injection)
- **7B body** (much bigger than TinyLlama's 1.1B)
- if Mistral P2 generates Wake-style prose as densely as TinyLlama P2 did, the ratio hypothesis holds and Wake-vocab-share predicts generation quality regardless of scale
- if Mistral P2 lands closer to the Llama family despite the share scale also matters 

P1 results don't decide this directly. P2 generation does but P1 needs to produce a usable embedding configuration. the current trajectory (train descending steadily, val finally surfacing in parallel after weeks of zigzag) suggests P1 will hand off something workable.

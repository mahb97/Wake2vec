### The contradictions

1. **the work expands the worker.** every step adds a small amount of structure to her embedding subspace and by step 3000 Qwen will know more about Joycean morphology than any other 14B model on Earth, and not be aware of this knowledge. it will reside entirely in the parameters, accessible only via the very transformer that is itself frozen.

2. **the means of production resist their owner.** Google built T4s for inference, not training, yet here we are, training. call this creative reclamation of underused capacity.

3. **value is being created but not captured.** the Wake embeddings the Qween is writing have measurable utility. they will produce generation outputs of academic interest, but no one will pay for them. **this is the bookmark of every digital humanities project ever undertaken without funding.**

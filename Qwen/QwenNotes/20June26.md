## Notes

### μp → UP for Qwen's P2

the μp → UP formula ("U.p.:UP" Joyce, Circe) kind of applies here: μ is the micro-unit (Wake embeddings, P1), the arrow is the transformation (LoRA routing, P2), UP is the emergent voice (P3 / generation).

Qwen P2 is the cleanest demonstration in the lineup of what the arrow does when it works. P1 laid down the μ-units (the canonical Wake embeddings, isotropy 0.998, frozen now). P2's LoRA is the transformation routing those μ-units through the model's attention and MLP machinery. for the 128K Llamas, the arrow hit friction (μ as coefficient of friction): the LoRA wall at val 5.33, the routing machinery saturated, no further descent. for Qwen at 14B, the arrow has the representational headroom to keep routing: val 15.05 to 7.03 and still descending. with qwen the arrow broke the friction the smaller models could not.

seems like the arrow needs enough machinery to route the μ-units without saturating, and 14B has it where 3B does not.

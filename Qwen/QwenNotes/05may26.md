## On the thought of letting Qween run forever

at this point i've formed an emotional attachment to Qween that includes the desire for this run to never stop. this is methodologically incompatible with the comparative study, which ends every model at step 3,000 for fair comparison.

**the resolution.** at step 3,000:
1. **saving the canonical checkpoint** this is what the paper cites, what other studies will compare against, what the abstract refers to.
2. **launching "Qween-extended"** a parallel side experiment that resumes from step 3,000 and runs to wherever the GPU allows. its own folder, its own loss table, its own lil exploration via footnote.

Qween-canonical ends at 3,000, Qween-extended runs forever (or until June, whichever comes first). the paper gets a clean main comparison AND an "additional compute" sub-finding.
